About Me

Hi there! I'm Lyn A, a passionate Front-End Developer based in Oslo, Norway.

My journey into the world of web development started with a curiosity for how websites are built and a desire to create engaging user experiences. I enjoy translating designs into functional, interactive, and visually appealing web applications.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am continuously learning and expanding my skill set. I have experience working with various front-end frameworks and libraries, and I'm particularly interested in responsive design and accessibility.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the latest trends in web development, participating in coding challenges, or contributing to open-source projects. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on new challenges.
